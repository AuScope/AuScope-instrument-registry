{{ _("Quick Start") }}

To register your instruments with a new organisation in the repository:

  1. Log in via (AAF)
  2. Submit a request to create a instrument organisation
  3. You will be notified via email after the organisation is created
  4. Select your organisation and choose:
    • Add Instrument(register individual instruments via the online form)

To register your instruments with an existing organisation in the repository:

  1. Log in via (AAF)
  2. Select your organisation-of-interest
  3. Click
    • Add Instrument(register individual instruments via the online form)
